About Me

I'm a data scientist working in medical imaging, with a keen interest in building and improving models for analyzing medical imaging. I have experience with neural networks, deep learning, and computer vision, developing AI models that help with tasks like diagnostics and automation in healthcare as well as other industries. My work involves training and fine-tuning models to make them more accurate and reliable for real-world use.
